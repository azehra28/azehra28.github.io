Summer Shows I Loved

Music is how I bookmark memories, and here are three concerts from this summer that I’ll never forget.

  • The Weeknd - Chicago: We’d bought tickets eight months in advance, and hearing our playlist live felt like the perfect ending to college, a night of nonstop singing with my closest friends.
  • Coldplay - Madison: My best friend and I met in sixth-grade piano class practicing “Clocks,” so seeing Coldplay together felt full-circle. Experiencing it in our college football stadium, the same place we graduated, made it even more meaningful.
  • Atif Aslam - Chicago: My Pakistani friends and I did a quick day trip to Chicago to de-stress with our favorite desi singer, and we sang our hearts out.
