Hotpot

I love bold, spicy flavors, and hotpot is my happy place. Back in Pakistan, there’s a popular spot called Wang Wang that I’m obsessed with. It is perfect for long dinners with friends and endless toppings. Here’s what my typical order looks like:

  1. Chinese noodles
  2. Thinly sliced Chinese beef
  3. Sausages
  4. Broccoli
  5. Corn pieces
  6. Chicken dumplings
  7. Extra-hot broth pot

Quick Notes

Spice level
Usually “extra hot,” with chilli oil on the side.
Go-to add-ons
Garlic Cucumbers, Mushrooms, and Carrots.

Biryani

My second favorite is biryani, my ultimate comfort food and a homemade classic back home. I’m forever trying to recreate my mom’s exact recipe: perfectly spiced rice, tender meat, and that fragrant steam when the lid comes off, nothing feels more like home.
